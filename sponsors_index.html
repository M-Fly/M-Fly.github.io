Sponsorships

M-Fly relies on donations and corporate sponsorships to fund all of our operation throughout the school years. If you are interested in sponsoring our team, check out our Contact Us page. Your generously donated funds will go to the following team expenses:

  • Purchase materials for aircraft constructions
  • Design and test prototypes
  • Fund competition registration and travel
  • Host community outreach events
